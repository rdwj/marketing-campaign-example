# Social Media Content Plan

## Platform Strategy

### Primary: Instagram
- Visual platform suits product photos
- Nostalgia content performs well
- Reels can go viral
- Link in bio to Etsy shop

### Secondary: TikTok
- Highest viral potential
- "90s kid" content resonates
- Day-in-the-life selling content popular
- Younger audience but nostalgic for 90s (ironic appreciation)

### Optional: Facebook
- Beanie Baby collector groups exist
- Good for driving traffic
- Older demographic (actual 90s collectors)

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. The Journey (40%)
Document the liquidation process. People love a story.

- Day 1 photo: "1,543 Beanie Babies. Once destined for millions. Let's see what actually happens."
- Weekly updates: "Week 4: 47 sold, 1,496 to go"
- Behind-the-scenes: photographing, packing, shipping

### 2. Individual Features (30%)
Spotlight specific Beanie Babies with personality.

- Fun facts about each one
- "Meet [Name]" posts
- Price history (what we thought vs. reality)
- Rare finds when you list them

### 3. Nostalgia Content (20%)
Tap into shared 90s memories.

- "POV: It's 1998 and..."
- Tag protector jokes
- "That one friend who cut the tags" memes
- Price guide screenshots from the era

### 4. Educational/Useful (10%)
Build credibility and provide value.

- "How to tell if your Beanie Baby is rare"
- "What are tag generations?"
- "Why PVC vs PE matters"
- Tips for other sellers

---

## Weekly Posting Schedule

### Minimum Viable Posting
- **3 posts per week**
- **1 Story per day** (low effort, high engagement)

### Ideal Posting (if time allows)
- **5 posts per week**
- **2-3 Stories per day**
- **1 Reel per week**

## Story Ideas (Daily Low-Effort Content)

### Engagement Stories
- Poll: "Did your family collect Beanie Babies?"
- Quiz: "Can you name this Beanie Baby?"
- Question box: "What was your favorite?"

### Behind-the-Scenes
- Photo station setup
- Packing orders
- Sorting inventory
- Tag close-ups

### Countdown/Progress
- Daily sold counter
- "X days until 50% sold"
- Order notifications (with privacy)

### Repurposed Content
- Share customer reviews
- Repost user content (if they tag you)
- Share throwback 90s content from other accounts

## Engagement Strategy

### Respond to Comments
- Reply to every comment within 24 hours
- Be friendly and on-brand
- Answer questions, accept compliments graciously
- Don't argue with negativity

### Engage with Others
- Follow/engage with other Beanie Baby sellers (community, not competition)
- Comment on 90s nostalgia content
- Engage in collector groups/communities

### User-Generated Content
- Ask buyers to share photos of their purchases
- Repost with permission
- Create a branded hashtag for buyers

## Metrics to Track

### Weekly Check
- Follower count
- Post reach/impressions
- Engagement rate (likes + comments / followers)
- Profile visits
- Link clicks

### Monthly Review
- Best performing posts (replicate what works)
- Follower growth rate
