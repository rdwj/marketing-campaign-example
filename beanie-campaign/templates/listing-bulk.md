## Condition Categories for Bulk

### Excellent Bulk (Premium Pricing +20%)
- 80%+ have tags
- Tags in good condition
- No visible wear or damage
- Clean, no odors

### Standard Bulk (Base Pricing)
- 50-80% have tags
- Some tag wear acceptable
- Minor cosmetic wear on some pieces
- All clean, no damage

### As-Is Bulk (Discounted -20%)
- Under 50% have tags
- Some missing eyes/noses (noted)
- More visible wear
- Still no stains/odors/rips

## Pro Tips for Bulk Lots

1. **Be honest about condition** - Undersell slightly; happy buyers leave reviews
2. **Calculate shipping carefully** - Bulk is heavy; don't lose money on shipping
3. **Photograph representative sample** - Don't cherry-pick best pieces for photo
4. **Offer volume discounts** - Incentivize buying multiple lots
5. **Consider local pickup** - Large lots can save on shipping if buyer is nearby
6. **Bundle shipping** - "Buy 2 lots, free shipping" moves more inventory
