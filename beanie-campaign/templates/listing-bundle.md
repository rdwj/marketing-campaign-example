## Pro Tips for Bundles

1. **Mix popular with less popular** - Include one or two recognizable names with less common pieces
2. **Vary sizes/shapes** - Visual interest makes better photos
3. **Keep themes clear** - Don't stretch themes too thin
4. **Note any standouts** - If one piece in the bundle is notably more desirable, mention it
5. **Photograph attractively** - Bundles sell on the group photo
